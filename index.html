After pickup

Cake Care Guide.

🤲

Carry the cake from the bottom only

⚖️

Keep it level when moving or refrigerating

🚗

Transport in an air-conditioned car away from loose objects

🌡️

Store in the fridge until ready to serve

☀️

Keep away from sunlight, moisture & heat

⏰

Take out of fridge 1 hour before eating

🎂

Use a cake stand at least 1 inch wider than the cake

🥡

Store leftovers in the fridge in an airtight container
